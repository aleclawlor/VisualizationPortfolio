Welcome to my Visualization Portfolio

This site is a collection of the Visualizations I have made so far in my coding career, beginning with those made for my class in Data Visualization.

*please click on Visualization titles to view

World Sustainability Exploration (2019)


An exploration of Sustainability metrics around the world. Consists of an interactive map showing sustainability margins and a stacked bar chart showing biodiversity data for nearly all countries around the world. Worked on as part of a team.



Examining the U.S. Housing Crisis (2019)


A look into the United States housing crisis and how people have been affected by rising housing prices over time, including a look into how the current job market provides housing uncertainty. Worked on as a team project.



EagleMUNC Registration (2020)


An analysis of registration data for the eigth iteration of the Model United Nations conference EagleMUNC as well as data throughout more past conferences. Created as a visual report for the registration period. Worked on individually.
